About the Artist

Kaitlyn Zinnel is currently a student at Northwest Missouri State University. She is a sophomore studying to obtain a Graphic Design degree with a Digital Media minor. Kaitlyn's work is influenced by illustrative styles, and she enjoys responding to the challenge of different mediums and prompts.

This site was created as part of the Web Development I course at Northwest Missouri State University and was developed to learn and enhance the concepts within that course. It is not intended for public use.
